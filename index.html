About TenderConnect

TenderConnect is designed to enhance transparency and reduce bureaucracy in the tender management process. Our platform allows institutions to post tenders and suppliers to apply seamlessly, ensuring secure user authentication and document handling.

With a robust backend powered by PostgreSQL and Express, TenderConnect ensures fast and reliable service, making tender management easy and efficient for all parties involved.
